The following agenda outlines what will be taught on the course.

The software prerequisites for the lessons can be found here. There are details on this page about a help day on October 27 to assist with any installation problems. Please ensure you have followed the software prerequisite instructions to install all of the necessary software by this date.

The course will run each Thursday morning for 8 weeks, with the first lesson on October 29 and the final one on December 17. The course training materials (which you can revisit and follow at your leisure) are linked to from the topics below. The schedule for the course is as follows:
