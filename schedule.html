You must install the software needed for the lessons by 26 October 2020. Instructions can be found on the software prerequisites page. We will provide helpers online all day on 26th October to help with any installation problems (further details about this day are also provided on the software prerequisites page.

The course will run each Thursday morning for 8 weeks, with the first lesson on 29 October and the final one on 17 December. The course training materials (which you can revisit and follow at your leisure) are linked to from the topics below. The schedule for the course is as follows:

Seminars will take place following each of the lessons. There will be two groups.

  • Group 2:Bazaga Martinez, Bilsby, Cheung, Coke, Fisher, Haime, Haszczyn, Jones, Jones, Lovegrove, Penman, Pidlisna, Rahimi and Ulrich
